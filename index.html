Installing the Spotify Web Player Extension

Once you're well-versed in the features and keyboard shortcuts, installing the Spotify Web Player Chrome extension is time. Moreover, follow these simple steps to add this tool to your browser effortlessly:

  • Start Installation Firstly, click the "Add to Chrome" button to initiate the installation process.
  • Confirm Installation Then, click the “Add Extension” button on your screen to proceed.
  • Access Your Extensions Find the “Puzzle” icon on the Chrome toolbar's right-hand side and click it to view your extensions.
  • Secure the Extension Afterwards, pin the extension by clicking the pin icon next to its name, and ensure it turns blue to keep it easily accessible.
  • Enjoy the Extension Complete the setup by starting your music on the official Spotify website and controlling it via the Mini Player extension.
